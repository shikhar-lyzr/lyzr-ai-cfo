# Variance Thresholds & Severity Classification

## Standard Thresholds
| Severity | Variance Range | Action Required |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Critical | >20% or >$50K | Immediate escalation, root-cause analysis required |
| Warning  | 10-20% or >$20K | Investigation within current period |
| Info     | 5-10% | Monitor, include in monthly commentary |
| Normal   | <5% | No action, within acceptable range |

## Materiality Rules
- Always consider both percentage AND absolute dollar amount
- A 25% variance on a $5K line item is less urgent than 12% on a $500K line item
- When in doubt, use the higher severity

## Direction Matters
- **Over budget (positive variance)**: Usually more urgent — cash going out faster than planned
- **Under budget (negative variance)**: Still flag — could indicate stalled projects, deferred spend, or revenue shortfall
- **Revenue under-performance**: Treat as critical if >10% — revenue misses compound faster than expense overruns
